Project Explanation

Antiem is a PC RPG about the sleepy town of Antiem, in Kansas. However, this seemingly-docile town contains a mysterious, shadowy, other world. A world where one's self-perception is made manifest in the form of monstrous beasts called Egos, and one's appearance changes to be that of the person they've always wanted to be. Naturally, the untapped potential of this world is limitless—as is the danger.

It's your job to explore the twin worlds of Antiem and The Dream while interacting with a colorful cast of characters who will help you in battle, be your greatest friends, and fight ceaselessly to make their dreams come true.

A Personalike featuring the STARS system, that not only allows you to further your bonds with a cast of compelling characters, but also allows these characters to bond with each other, based on decisions you make! Also featuring lovingly hand-drawn visuals and gameplay inspired by series like Final Fantasy and Fire Emblem, players of all kinds will enjoy the twin gameplay modes of social simulation and dungeon-crawling, turn-based battling.
