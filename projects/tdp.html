Project Explanation

Twitch Dates Pokémon is a family-friendly visual novel that plays on and parodies many of the long-running tropes and themes of other visual novels, based around the lore of the 2014 internet phenomenon Twitch Plays Pokémon.

Take up the role of Burrito, an Espeon and transfer student to Twitch Academy, as you navigate your way through the tangled social webs, mysteries, and secrets that this game's cast of colorful characters weave around you.

Featuring seven dateable characters, and dozens of endings, your decisions will matter. Will you graduate from Twitch Academy a success? Will you have found love? Or will you drop out, and leave the best years of your 'mon-life behind you? It's up to you. But in this game, failure isn't necessarily the end...
