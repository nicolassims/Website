Project Explanation

Minecraft is a game that requires almost no introduction. Being a survival sandbox experience, at its very core, its combination of simple but appealing graphics and timeless gameplay loops have let it claim the throne of the most-sold video game of all time.

The two primary editions of Minecraft, Java and Bedrock, have some differences, with Bedrock edition being the version that is cross-platform, and enjoyed by the widest array of consumers. Super League Gaming acquired the company known as InPvP, who currently owns two of the official Minecraft Bedrock edition servers, which exist with Microsoft's explicit partnership.

Boasting a wide array of minigames, as well as large open areas to inspire roleplaying, Pixel Paradise and Mineville, InPvP's two servers, have a enough varieties of entertainment to satisfy any demographic or audience.
