Project Explanation

The Rhodes Island Experiences is a 4-koma webcomic based on the characters and scenarios of the video game Arknights.

Taking a much more lighthearted approach to the world of Terra than the game does, The Rhodes Island Experiences is a slice-of-life comedy about the misadventures of characters like Projekt Red, the innocent assassin with a heart of gold, Vigna, the soldier who dreams of being a rock star, and SilverAsh, the cutthroat businessman with a terrible crush.

Nhim_Art, the artist, posts a comic almost every single day, starting on 2/21/2020, and--as of the time of writing this, on 7/13/2021--has released over 404 4-panel comics, not including special high-effort paintings that mark the end of each story arc.
