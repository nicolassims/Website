Project Explanation

Pokémon Academy Life: Forever is a PC/Mac/Android Visual Novel that takes place in the familiar world of Pokémon--if, in the Pokémon world, you had to go to school to become a trainer, and ten-year-olds weren't just released into the wild to fend for themselves.

In PAL:F, you learn to manage your time between attending classes, forming bonds with people, passing tests, and catching and training an ever-expanding pool of Pokémon. The writing takes full advantage of the college setting to present players with mature storylines and problems--like how to pay for higher education.

A Personalike featuring an accurate recreation of the infamously complex Pokémon battle system, also featuring lovingly hand-drawn visuals and hundreds of character portraits, players of all kinds will enjoy the twin gameplay modes of social simulation and dungeon-crawling, turn-based battling.
